About me

O meni

My name is Martin Oprin and I am a student at the Vegova Ljubljana. I am currently studying computer science and I am in my last year of studies. I am interested in web development and I am currently learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. I am also interested in networking and cybersecurity. In my free time, I started flying FPV-drones and I am currently learning to DJ.

Moje ime je Martin Oprin in sem dijak srednje šole Vegova Ljubljana. Trenutno študiram računalništvo in sem v zadnjem letniku šolanja. Zanimam me razvoj spletnih aplikacij in trenutno se učim HTML, CSS, JavaScript in PHP. Prav tako me zanimam za kibernetsko varnost. V prostem času se učim letenja z FPV dronom in v prihodnosti bi rad postal DJ na manjših zabavah.
